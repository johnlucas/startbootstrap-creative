There was a time when I felt that hearing loss was holding me back. Then I got hearing aids, but my journey didn't end there: I had to work hard to feel like hearing loss was no longer an obstacle in my life.

Wearing hearing aids can be challenging

Hi! I'm Gianluca. I grew up with moderately-severe hearing loss but it wasn't until my twenties that I decided to buy hearing aids. That choice changed my life for the better, but I was also faced with new challenges: everything sounded louder and even with the help of hearing aids I was still struggling to hear at times.

More than a decade of learnings

Despite the challenges, I pushed myself to live the life I wanted. Outside of my comfort zone. I lived in and traveled to many different countries and I learned to speak english and spanish fluently.
Today you can find me in London working as a product manager, a communicator role. I spend a lot of time in meetings and still get things done, even though I can't hear well all the time.

But it shouldn't take that long...

Even though I'm happy now, I wish I had more support along the way and that the journey to successfully living with hearing loss had been shorter and easier for me.
